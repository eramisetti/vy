HEALTH, HAPPINESS & HARMONY
Vivekananda Yoga of San Diego is a registered non-profit organization 501(c) (3) with IRS. EIN# 83-3066629. Primary purpose is to promote healthy lifestyle in local community by conducting introductory Yoga seminars and classes for adults and children.
  • Conduct children classes to promote healthy living, wellness education, value of physical exercises and mindful eating habits.
  • Conduct classes for adults to promote stress reduction, healthy living, yoga exercises and wellness.
  • Spread the awareness about benefits of Yoga, active and healthy lifestyle
  • Invite experts in the fields to train volunteers for promoting healthy living and practice of Yoga.
  • To promote mindfulness among adults and children through Yoga
