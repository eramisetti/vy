Methodology

Yoga Postures, Pranayama, Guided Meditation, Games, Songs and stories

What do kids get from the yoga

Physical fitness:

Strength, stamina, flexibility, dexterity, posture, gait and confident and graceful looks.

Mental fitness:

Improves concentration, calms down the mind. Children experience the joy and freedom

Emotional culturing:

Cultivating the positive emotions like love, compassion, confidence etc and weed out negative emotions like jealousy, anger, fear, anxiety, Discriminatory Faculties (Budhi) , Social skills (leadership, Decision making, goal setting etc) and respect to our Culture.
