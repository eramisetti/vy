OUR VISION

Our vision is to promote a healthy lifestyle, reducing stress through mindfulness and yoga.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to spread awareness of the benefits of yoga, active lifestyle and mindfulness to achieve a healthy balanced lifestyle by conducting classes, seminars for both adults and children.

OUR VALUES

We meet people where they inspire themselves towards healing, evolution, and personal growth.

What we do
Through the means of Yoga, we help to train body and mind to create awareness of our own nature in an easy to understand step by step process. Classes focus on Asana, Pranayama, relaxation and meditation techniques. Yoga philosophy and other deeper aspects of Ashtanga Yoga are explored in advanced and Yoga instructors classes. Training includes review of challenges in this Yoga journey, introduction to best practices to overcome these challenges, tips for effective diet and suggestions for making lifestyle changes.
